What is Coding?

Coding is like writing a super detailed recipe for a computer to follow. Computers are really fast at doing work, but they aren't smart enough to know what to do on their own. When you write code, you are giving the computer step-by-step directions—telling it when to jump, make a sound, or display a picture on the screen!

Just like people speak different languages like English or Spanish, computers have special languages, too! Some coding languages, like Scratch, use colorful blocks you snap together like LEGO bricks. Others, like Python or JavaScript, use typed words and numbers. No matter which one you use, they all help us talk to the computer so it understands our instructions.

With code, you get to be a digital creator! You can use it to build your very own video games, design websites, make cool phone apps, or even tell a real-life robot how to drive around and dance. If you can imagine an idea, coding gives you the magic powers to make it come to life on a screen!
